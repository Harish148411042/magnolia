Dr. SHARAT D HEGDE

MBBS | DNB

Consultant - Internal Medicine

​Dr. Sharat D Hegde is a seasoned Internal Medicine specialist with over 22 years of experience, currently practicing at Astra Superspeciality Hospital in Bangalore. He completed his MBBS from BLDEA's Shri B M Patil Medical College in Bijapur in 2002 and obtained his DNB in General Medicine from the National Board of Examinations in 2009. Throughout his career, Dr. Hegde has been associated with several reputable hospitals, including The Bangalore Hospital and Excelcare Hospital. His clinical expertise encompasses a wide range of services such as health check-ups, vaccination and immunization, allergy treatment, and management of infectious diseases. He is also skilled in procedures like upper GI endoscopy and ERCP. Dr. Hegde's commitment to patient care and his extensive experience make him a trusted physician in the field of internal medicine.

Dr. PRAVEEN KUMAR

MBBS | DM

Consultant - Neurophysician

Dr. S. Praveen Kumar is a highly experienced Consultant Neurophysician with over 20 years in neurology. He completed his MBBS from Bangalore Medical College in 2002 and DM in Neurology from NIMHANS in 2009. He has worked with reputed hospitals like Dr. Malathi Manipal Hospital, Sri Sai Ram Hospital, and Rangadore Memorial Hospital. A member of IAN, ISA, and MDSI, his expertise includes strokes, spinal cord disorders, epilepsy, headaches, movement disorders, and neuropathies. He has received the Neurology Young Scholar Award (2010) and Best Paper Presentation in Neurology (2007) and has over 20 research publications

Dr. PREETHAM H N

MBBS | DNB

Consultant - ENT Specialist

​Dr. Preetham H N is an experienced ENT/Otorhinolaryngologist practicing at Pathway Hospitals in Rajarajeshwarinagar, Bangalore. He completed his MBBS from Kasturba Medical College in 2011 and obtained a Diploma in Otorhinolaryngology (DLO) from Karnataka Institute of Medical Sciences, Hubli, in 2017. With over 14 years of experience, including 7 years as a specialist, Dr. Preetham is proficient in procedures such as balloon sinus ostial dilation (balloon sinuplasty), canalplasty, migraine treatment, orbital and optic nerve decompression, and turbinate reduction surgery. Additionally, he holds a DNB in ENT, further enhancing his expertise in the field
