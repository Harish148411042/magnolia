Our Services

Rehabilitation Programs

Rehabilitation programs focus on restoring physical strength, cognitive function, and emotional well-being after injury, illness, or surgery. They include physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and psychological support tailored to individual needs. These programs help patients regain mobility, improve daily functioning, and enhance their quality of life. Specialized rehabilitation is available for stroke recovery, post-surgical care, neurological conditions, and musculoskeletal disorders.

Neuro Rehabilitation

Neuro rehabilitation is a specialized program designed to help individuals recover from neurological conditions such as stroke, brain injury, spinal cord disorders, and multiple sclerosis. It includes physiotherapy, speech therapy, cognitive therapy, and occupational therapy to improve mobility, coordination, and overall brain function. The goal is to enhance independence, restore lost abilities, and improve the patient’s quality of life. Personalized treatment plans ensure targeted support based on the severity of the condition

Nephro Rehabilitation

Nephro rehabilitation is a supportive program designed for patients with chronic kidney disease (CKD) or those recovering from kidney-related conditions. It focuses on lifestyle modifications, dietary management, and physical therapy to enhance kidney function and overall well-being. The program also includes patient education on dialysis, medication adherence, and strategies to prevent disease progression. Regular monitoring and a multidisciplinary approach help improve the patient’s quality of life and reduce complications

Orthopaedic Rehabilitation

Orthopaedic rehabilitation is a specialized therapy program designed for patients recovering from musculoskeletal injuries, fractures, or surgeries like joint replacements. It focuses on pain management, mobility restoration, and strengthening exercises to improve overall function. The program includes physiotherapy, manual therapy, and assistive techniques to enhance flexibility and reduce stiffness. Personalized treatment plans help patients regain independence and prevent future injuries

Alzheimer’s Rehabilitation

Alzheimer’s rehabilitation focuses on improving cognitive function, maintaining daily living skills, and enhancing the quality of life for patients with Alzheimer’s disease. It includes memory exercises, physical therapy, and structured routines to slow cognitive decline. Caregivers are also trained to provide emotional and practical support. The goal is to ensure a safe, comfortable environment while promoting independence for as long as possible

Stroke Management

Stroke management involves immediate medical intervention, rehabilitation, and long-term care to aid recovery and prevent complications. Rehabilitation focuses on restoring mobility, speech, and cognitive functions through physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy. Lifestyle modifications, including a healthy diet, exercise, and medication, help reduce the risk of recurrent strokes. Emotional and psychological support is also essential for improving the patient’s overall well-being and independence

Senior Care & Assisted Living

Senior Care & Assisted Living provides personalized support for elderly individuals who require daily assistance with activities like bathing, dressing, and medication management. It includes medical supervision, nutritious meals, social engagement, and a safe living environment to enhance their quality of life. These services promote independence while ensuring access to healthcare and companionship. Specialized care is also available for seniors with conditions like dementia or mobility challenges

Transitional Care

Transitional Care focuses on ensuring a smooth shift from hospital to home or another healthcare setting by providing continued medical supervision and rehabilitation support. It helps reduce hospital readmissions by offering personalized care plans, medication management, and therapy services. The goal is to enhance recovery, improve patient outcomes, and support families in managing post-hospitalization care. This service is especially beneficial for individuals recovering from surgery, chronic illness, or major medical events like strokes

Continuum Care

Continuum Care provides seamless and coordinated healthcare services across different stages of a patient's journey, ensuring consistent medical support. It includes preventive care, acute treatment, rehabilitation, and long-term management to enhance overall well-being. This approach helps patients receive personalized care without disruption, improving recovery and quality of life. It is particularly beneficial for those with chronic conditions, elderly patients, and individuals requiring long-term healthcare assistance

Palliative Care

Palliative care focuses on improving the quality of life for individuals with serious or life-limiting illnesses by managing pain, symptoms, and emotional distress. It provides holistic support, including medical, psychological, and spiritual care, tailored to the patient’s needs. This care can be given alongside curative treatments or as end-of-life support. The goal is to ensure comfort, dignity, and enhanced well-being for patients and their families

Physiotherapy OPD

Magnolia’s Physiotherapy OPD offers specialized outpatient physiotherapy services to help patients recover from injuries, surgeries, and musculoskeletal conditions. Our experienced physiotherapists provide personalized treatment plans, including manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and pain management techniques. The focus is on restoring mobility, improving strength, and enhancing overall physical function. Whether it's post-surgical rehabilitation or chronic pain management, our OPD services ensure effective and continuous care for all patients

Laboratory Services

Magnolia’s Laboratory Services offer state-of-the-art diagnostic testing to ensure accurate and timely health assessments. Our advanced lab facilities provide a wide range of tests, including blood tests, urine analysis, and specialized screenings for various medical conditions. With highly skilled technicians and cutting-edge technology, we ensure precise results to aid in early diagnosis and effective treatment planning. Our services are designed for patient convenience, offering quick turnaround times and reliable reports

Magnolia’s Sleep Study services help diagnose and manage sleep disorders such as sleep apnea, insomnia, and restless leg syndrome. Using advanced monitoring techniques, we assess brain activity, breathing patterns, heart rate, and oxygen levels during sleep. Our expert team analyzes the results to create personalized treatment plans for better sleep quality and overall health. With a focus on patient comfort, we ensure a seamless and accurate diagnostic experience

Post Surgical Care

Post-surgical care is a critical aspect of recovery that involves comprehensive management to prevent complications and ensure optimal healing. It includes wound care, pain management, infection prevention, and rehabilitation through physiotherapy if necessary. Monitoring vital signs, managing medications, and providing nutritional support are essential to promote tissue repair and immune function. Depending on the type of surgery, specialized care may involve drain management, mobility assistance, or respiratory therapy to prevent issues like pneumonia or blood clots. Patient education on self-care, recognizing warning signs of complications, and regular follow-ups with the healthcare provider play a key role in successful recovery.
